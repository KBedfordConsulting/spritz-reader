Spritz Reader

A speed reading app for EPUBs and PDFs. Words appear one at a time at a fixed focal point, so your eyes stay put while text flows past. Most people read 2–3x faster this way.

Once your file loads, use the controls at the bottom to play, pause, and skip forward or back. Adjust your reading speed with the slider. Open the table of contents from the menu in the upper left to jump between chapters.
